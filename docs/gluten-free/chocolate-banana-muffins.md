# Banana chocolate chip muffins

## Ingredients

* 1 1/2 cup flour
* 1/3 cup sugar
* 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
* 1/2 tsp salt
* 3 bananas
* 1 egg
* 1/2 cup melted unsalted butter
* 1 tsp vanilla
* 1/4 cup milk
* 1 cup chocolate chips

## Preparation

Bake at 425F for 5 min; reduce to 350F for another 15-20 min
