# Cinnamon buns

Makes 9 large buns

## Ingredients

### Dough

* 3/4 cup warm milk (~110F)
* 2 1/4 tsp yeast
* 1/4 cup white sugar
* 1 egg + 1 yolk, room temp
* 1/4 cup unsalted melted butter
* 3 cups flour
* 3/4 tsp salt

### Filling

* 2/3 cup brown sugar
* 1 1/2 tbsp cinnamon
* 1/4 cup softened butter

## Preparation

* Combine milk and yeast.
* Add eggs, sugar, melted butter
* Stir in flour and salt
* Rise ~1.5 hours
